Sumit Ray - Aspiring Frontend Developer

Greetings! I am Sumit Ray, a passionate individual with a keen interest in frontend development. I completed my basic education at V.S Niketan School, where I laid the foundation for my future endeavors.

My journey into the professional world began with a six-month internship at Norvic Hospital, where I gained valuable insights and hands-on experience in a real-world setting. This opportunity honed my skills and provided a practical understanding of the dynamics of the workplace.

One of my proudest achievements is my involvement in a project where my teammate and I successfully collaborated on creating an innovative solution. We dived into the world of technology, utilizing an Arduino Mega and a NODE MCU temperature sensor. This project not only showcased my ability to work collaboratively but also my passion for turning ideas into reality.

Looking ahead, my goal is to specialize in frontend development. I am committed to enhancing user experiences through intuitive and visually appealing interfaces. I am enthusiastic about leveraging my skills to contribute to cutting-edge projects and staying at the forefront of technological advancements.

I am eager to continue expanding my knowledge and skills in the realm of frontend development. With a strong foundation, practical experience, and a commitment to continuous learning, I am poised to make meaningful contributions in the ever-evolving field of web development.
